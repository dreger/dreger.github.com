Big Cartel Guides

Big Cartel, the makers of a customizable online store for your creative goods, has a delightful micro-site that features small guides filled with “Practical advice for everything you may encounter throughout your life as an artist and entrepreneur.”

Each guide is short, informative, and well designed. My favorites were the ones on product photography, copyright, and taxes.
