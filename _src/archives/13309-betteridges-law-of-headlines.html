Betteridge's Law of Headlines

Speaking of dishonest reporting, Ian Betteridge has a simple way to quickly filter through most of the sensationalist news out there today:

Any headline which ends in a question mark can be answered by the word no.

Through personal experience, this has been proven correct time and time again.
