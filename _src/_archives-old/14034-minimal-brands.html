Minimizing Brands

Mehmet Gozetlik, the founder of design collective Antrepo, has taken 17 well-known brands and reduced their products' branding to the bare minimal. Although I think consumers would quickly tire of everything being set in Helvetica, I do like how Gozetlik favored printing directly on the glass or plastic instead of using a paper wrapper.
