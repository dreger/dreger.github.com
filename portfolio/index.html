Hello, my name is Kyle Dreger. For the past nine years, I've had a love for user experience, typography, and clean, functional design.

I am currently in my senior year at the University of Mount Union, double majoring in computer science and writing. I work at the Center for Writing and Oral Communication where I teach students effective ways to write and speak.

For the past two summers, I've interned at The Timken Company in the New Technology department. At Timken, I helped usher in a new web framework for rapid web application development and lead the design for multiple iPad web applications.

Here is some of my web and design work:

On Tap Grille and Bar

When owner Mike Simms asked me to help him develop a website for his restaurant, I was excited to try my hand at a single-page design. Drawing inspiration from minimalist menus, strong typography, and backing it all up with a functional CMS system, On Tap Grille and Bar website works great on all devices.

  • Responsive design
  • HTML5, CSS3 (SASS)
  • Photoshop and Illustrator CS5
  • Perch CMS installation and setup
  • PHP and MySQL

Website

Marquette School of Dance

The Marquette School of Dance has a long history of being energetic and inviting to dancers of all ages. After meeting with the owner and lead instructor, Missy Marquette, we developed a color palette and a design that would mirror these sentiments to all her students and visitors.

  • HTML5, CSS3 (SASS)
  • Photoshop and Illustrator CS5
  • Perch CMS installation and setup
  • PHP and MySQL
  • Custom web typography (Museo Sans Rounded)

Website

Cuyahoga Falls United Presbyterian Church

When the CFUPC Outreach Committee told me they were looking for a fresh design to bring their website up to speed, I couldn’t resist. This particular website had to be usable for an audience of all ages and needed to be accessible from a desktop, tablet, and mobile phone.

  • Responsive design
  • HTML5, CSS3 (SASS), JavaScript
  • Photoshop and Illustrator CS5
  • Perch CMS installation and setup
  • PHP and MySQL
  • Custom web typography (Proxima Nova)

Website

Mount Union Calliope

The advisors of Calliope, the University of Mount Union’s literary journal, were looking for a simple way to update their online web presence. Working closely with the head advisor, I helped design and implement a simple CMS system, Statamic, to allow for easy distribution of their latest work and publications.

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Photoshop CS5
  • Statamic CMS installation and setup
  • PHP and MySQL

KAO: Kent State University ATC
