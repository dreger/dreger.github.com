World Press Photo of the Year

Out of 82,951 photos, from 5,775 photographers, an image from Australian photographer Warren Richardson has been selected as World Press Photo of the Year. His photo, taken at the Hungarian-Serbian border, depicts a man helping pass a baby underneath the border’s razor wire fence. Such a haunting image. Be sure to check out the gallery of other winners, and scroll down beneath each photo to see the GPS, camera, and technical details of the shot.
