Quietly shipped alongside all that was released on Tuesday, iOS 7.0.3 is now available for download.

In addition to iCloud Keychain, which lets you sync your passwords between iOS devices, Apple has also addressed complaints regarding the system-wide parallax effects. Federico Viticci for MacStories:
