Mactracker.app

Writing my last post, I was trying to remember the name of an app I had used that listed the details and specifications of not just every Mac, but also every Apple device ever made. A little searching and I finally found it: Mactracker by Ian Page.

Page has versions of Mactracker available for OS X and iOS, and both versions are completely free. This is a fantastic little app, and it provides a great way to look at Apple’s history of products, in entirety, from the beginning to the present.
