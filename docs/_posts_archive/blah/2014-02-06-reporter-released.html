Reporter for iPhone Released

Earlier this morning, Reporter for iPhone was officially released on the App Store. Reporter, the brainchild of data-analyst Nicholas Feltron, randomly presents you with lightweight surveys throughout the day, and then compiles that data into fascinating personal reports. At just $3.99 cheap, this will be one of the first apps I buy when I am off my [digital media hiatus]({% post_url 2014-01-15-things-i-could-have-bought %}).
