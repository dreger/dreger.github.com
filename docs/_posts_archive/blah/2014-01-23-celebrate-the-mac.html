Celebrate The Mac

Tomorrow will mark 30 years since Steve Jobs first unveiled the original Macintosh (video). To help celebrate, Jonathan Zufi, the man behind the ICONIC Book I linked to a while back, has created a website called Celebrate The Mac where he has uploaded high-quality photographs of nearly every Mac from the past 30 years. Personally, my favorites are from his “classic” collection.

Compared to some of the competitors of the time, it is not hard to see why the Macintosh’s design was considered beautiful.
